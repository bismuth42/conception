# Book Recommendation App

## This app will recommend books based on genres and themes.

This app will have multiple categorgies based on authors, genre, timeline, etc. The user can choose from these categorgies based on their personal taste. 

Based on the previous choices made by the user, the app will recommend similar books.

Users can have their own personlised book lists with a rating scale. Also, users can buy a hard copy of the books through the affiliate link.
